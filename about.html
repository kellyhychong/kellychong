About Me

BMOS @ Western University / Designer ✒️

I love being inspired and inspiring others.

My name is Kelly Chong, and I am a first-year student at the University of Western Ontario in the BMOS program. Since high school, I've loved problem solving and creating positive impact through business and design. I strive to be able to express myself creatively and make a difference in my community every single day, for no greater joy arises from me than that of teaching others.

Currently, I'm a designer for Western Founders Network and PBSN's IMPACT series. I also sit on the Board of Directors for Fuse Society, a national non-profit organization dedicated to increasing business literacy in youth across Canada. In my free time I enjoy playing ultimate frisbee and exploring local hiking spots! Contact me at: kellyhychong@gmail.com
