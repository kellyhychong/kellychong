A little bit about me ⛰️

To seek adventure in the mundane. To engage with and give back to my community. To live my most authentic life possible, every day I can. Nice to meet you, I'm Kelly!

I'm a multidisciplinary digital creator and first-year student studying Business Management and Organizational Studies at Western University (Canada). After years of feeling like I could only restrict myself to a career path in business or design, I realized neither had to be compromised.

Now, I'm an aspiring product designer, having discovered my passions at the intersections of business, technology, and design. I also dabble in illustration, graphic design, and motion - but at the heart of it all, I love being able to problem-solve through design and make an impact on the world. I currently lead branding as the Co-VP Design at Western Founders Network, as well as design for one of Canada's largest hackathons - Hack Western 7.

When I'm not studying or fiddling with Figma, you can find me playing ultimate frisbee, singing with my ukulele, and reading Humble the Poet books!
