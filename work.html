Illustration

Windermere Marketing Group

A series of illustrations I created for the Windermere Marketing Group’s official website in 2018. These illustrations depict the three pillars of WMG: Warrior Warehouse, Events, and Professional Services. All vectors drawn on Adobe Illustrator.

16th Birthday

I created this short graphic as an experiment for celebrating one’s 16th birthday. Vectors drawn on Adobe Illustrator and animated on Adobe After Effects.

WMG Bubble Tea Sale

The following animated graphic was created to promote a bubble tea sale occurring at one of WMG’s annual events, the Back-to-School Festival. The published video garnered a reach of 1.3K views. Vectors drawn on Adobe Illustrator and animated on Adobe After Effects.

The Professionals of Tomorrow

Extensive typography project created to promote WMG’s annual event, “The Professionals of Tomorrow” (TPOT). The video reached a total of 1.6K views since release. Created using Adobe After Effects and plug-in Animation Composer. See the full video on YouTube here.
